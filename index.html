Real questions, honest answers

We're here to help you navigate those tricky conversations. Find answers to the most common questions parents and teens have as they explore these important topics together.

How do I start the first talk?

Pick a calm moment—maybe during a walk or while driving. Let your teen know you're open to any questions, and it's okay if things feel a bit awkward. Being honest about your feelings helps build trust.

What if my teen asks something I don't know?

It's totally fine not to have every answer. Tell your teen you'll figure it out together, and use the resources in the app to learn more. Showing you're willing to learn together makes a big difference.

How can I make these talks less uncomfortable?

Keep things light when you can—humor helps! Try using videos or stories to get started. Remind your teen that everyone feels a little awkward, but these talks show you care.

Is it okay to set boundaries?

Absolutely. Share what you're comfortable discussing and listen to your teen's boundaries, too. Respect goes both ways, and setting limits helps everyone feel safe and heard.
